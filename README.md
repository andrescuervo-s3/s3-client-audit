### s3-client-audit

Performs comprehensive client communication audits across five data sources (Slack, Gmail, Google Calendar, Notion, Google Drive), producing operational briefings that reveal where information is getting lost in your organization.

**Triggers:** "audit [client] channel", "how's [client] going?", "pull a report on [client]", "communication audit", "channel health check"

**Required Connectors:** Slack, Gmail, Google Calendar, Notion, Google Drive
